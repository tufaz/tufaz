Home Office Setup

Working from home can be as simple as sitting on the couch and using your laptop, but that is not condusive to getting work done on a consistent basis. So it is important to find out a workspace for the optimal balance between comfort and productivity. Here is a list of the most essential items you need
  1. Standing desk
  2. Ergonomic chair
  3. Ultrawide monitor
  4. Wireless keyboard & mouse
  5. Noise cancelling headphones
  6. Desk lamp
  7. Smart coffee maker
